Endangered Species Spotlight

Global biodiversity is declining at an alarming rate, putting the survival of other species and our own future at risk. WWFâ€™s 2016 Living Planet Report found global populations of fish, birds, mammals, amphibians and reptiles declined by 58% between 1970 and 2012. The objective of this project is to focus on 9 different species which are listed on the WWF website as endangered and create a visualization based on their habitat, threats and population. Each of the species play a crucial role in the ecosystem and their loss will trigger the loss of another species in that ecosystem.
